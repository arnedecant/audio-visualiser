Privacy Notice

This app uses your camera to visualize the video stream on a particle system. Each particle is animated according to depth and the audio played. This means this app needs both your permission to capture your camera and play music.

Some browsers require user interaction before any audio can be played. By clicking "okay" this app has the necessary user interaction to play music.

Important: I do not store anything from your camera on the server!

Important: Use Google Chrome for the best experience.
